About.

Hey, this is MIECS! Conceptualized by like-minded professionals from different segments of the society who have pledged for the upliftment and empowerment of tribal and rural society, particularly for the socially and economically vulnerable. It takes up various need based and context specific activities for the economic, social, cultural, educational and environmental development. The vision is to build a justifiable & equitable society, where all sections of the society enjoy a secure present and future, with special focus on the improved quality of life, livelihood pattern and poverty reduction.
